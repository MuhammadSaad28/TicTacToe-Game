Rules of Tic Tac Toe

Objective

The objective of Tic Tac Toe is to get three of your symbols (X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, on a 3x3 grid.

Gameplay

1. The game starts with an empty grid.

2. Player 1 places their symbol (X) on any empty box.

3. Player 2 places their symbol (O) on any empty box.

4. Players take turns until one player achieves a winning pattern or all boxes are filled.

Winning Patterns

There are eight possible winning patterns:

  • Horizontal: Three symbols in a row on the same line
  • Vertical: Three symbols in a column on the same line
  • Diagonal: Three symbols diagonally from one corner to another

Tips

  • Plan your moves strategically to block your opponent's winning patterns.
  • Look for opportunities to create your own winning patterns.
  • Pay attention to the positions of your opponent's symbols.
