About Tic Tac Toe

History

Tic Tac Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is an ancient game that dates back to ancient Egypt. It gained popularity over the years and became a beloved pastime for people of all ages.

Throughout history, Tic Tac Toe has been played in various forms and variations, but the objective has always remained the same: to get three of your symbols in a row.
